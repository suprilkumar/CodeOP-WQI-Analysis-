INDIA'S WATER QUALITY INSIGHTS:

A Data-Driven State-Wise Analysis

This project offers a comprehensive analysis of India's water quality using Central Pollution Control Board and Central Water Commission datasets, visualized with Power BI. It provides state-wise insights into parameters like eutrophication, pH, and fecal coliform levels, along with lists of polluted and safe water bodies, enabling better water management and public health strategies.

UN SDG-6: Clean Water and Sanitation

Our project aligns with the UN SDG 6 (Clean Water and Sanitation) by providing an in-depth analysis of India's water quality. Using CPCB and CWC datasets and Power BI visualizations, we offer state-wise insights on key water quality parameters, including eutrophication, pH levels, and fecal coliform, to support sustainable water management and public health.

Problem Statement

Ensuring access to clean water and sanitation is a global challenge, particularly in diverse and densely populated countries like India. Inadequate water quality management can lead to significant health and environmental issues. This project addresses this by delivering a centralized platform with detailed state-wise water quality analysis, utilizing CPCB and CWC data to enhance transparency, support effective decision-making, and contribute to achieving the UN SDG 6 goals.

Project Summary

Welcome to our interactive platform dedicated to analyzing and visualizing India's water quality across various states. Leveraging datasets from the Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB) and the Central Water Commission (CWC), and utilizing Power BI for advanced analysis and visualization, our site provides detailed insights into crucial water quality parameters. Explore interactive graphs and charts covering eutrophication levels, pH balance, and fecal coliform concentrations. Our state-wise water quality index and lists of polluted and safe water bodies will help you make informed decisions about water management and safety.

Project Outcomes

  • Comprehensive Data Visualization: Interactive Power BI dashboards presenting key water quality parameters such as eutrophication levels, pH balance, and fecal coliform concentrations.
  • State-Wise Analysis: Detailed water quality indices for each state, highlighting regions with critical water quality issues.
  • Polluted vs. Safe Water Bodies: Lists identifying polluted and safe water bodies in each state, aiding in targeted intervention and resource allocation.
  • Enhanced Decision-Making: Accessible, actionable data that supports effective water management strategies and public health initiatives.
